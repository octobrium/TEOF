### Script & Ideation

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Claude** | Thread writing, dialogue scripts, TEOF-aligned content | Best reasoning, follows voice docs, long-form coherence | Slower, more expensive |
| **GPT-4** | Quick brainstorms, hook variations, topic lists | Fast, creative variations | Can drift from voice |
| **Perplexity** | Research-backed content, fact-checking, trend research | Citations, current info | Not for creative writing |

**Decision tree:**
- Writing a thread or script? → Claude (load voice doc first)
- Need 10 hook variations fast? → GPT-4
- Need facts/stats for credibility? → Perplexity
- Combining research + writing? → Perplexity research → Claude writing

### Voice Generation

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **ElevenLabs** | Primary choice for all voice work | Best quality, voice cloning, multilingual | $22/mo for good tier |
| **Play.ht** | Backup, budget option | Cheaper, decent quality | Less natural than ElevenLabs |
| **Murf** | Corporate/professional tone | Clean, professional | Less character/emotion |

**Decision tree:**
- Philosopher debates, character voices? → ElevenLabs (clone historical voice samples)
- Narration, explainer content? → ElevenLabs or Murf
- Testing/prototyping? → Play.ht (cheaper)

### Video Generation

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **HeyGen** | Talking head avatars, "philosopher speaks" format | Best lip-sync, custom avatars | $24/mo, uncanny valley risk |
| **Synthesia** | Professional/corporate feel | Polished, enterprise-grade | Less creative flexibility |
| **D-ID** | Quick avatar tests, photo-to-video | Easy, fast | Lower quality than HeyGen |
| **Runway Gen-3** | Cinematic B-roll, atmospheric visuals | Best quality AI video | Expensive credits, slow |
| **Pika** | Motion graphics, stylized footage | Creative effects, fast | Less photorealistic |
| **Kling** | Longer clips, consistent motion | Better motion coherence | Newer, less tested |

**Decision tree:**
- Philosopher/character talking? → HeyGen (primary) or D-ID (quick test)
- Atmospheric B-roll (clouds, abstract, nature)? → Runway Gen-3
- Stylized/artistic footage? → Pika
- Need 10+ second continuous shot? → Kling

### Image Generation

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Midjourney** | Thumbnails, philosopher portraits, aesthetic scenes | Best aesthetics, most consistent | Discord-based (friction) |
| **DALL-E 3** | Quick iterations, text integration | Fast, good text rendering | Less artistic than MJ |
| **Flux** | Photorealistic, specific compositions | High realism | Newer, learning curve |

**Decision tree:**
- YouTube thumbnail? → Midjourney (dramatic, clickable)
- Image with text overlay? → DALL-E 3 (better text handling)
- Photorealistic scene? → Flux
- Quick concept test? → DALL-E 3

### Music Generation

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Suno** | Full songs, vocals, catchy hooks | Best for actual songs | Can be too "produced" |
| **Udio** | Background music, instrumental | Great instrumentals, moods | Less vocal capability |
| **Mubert** | Ambient, continuous background | Infinite generation, royalty-free | Generic, less memorable |

**Decision tree:**
- Debate background music? → Udio (tension, philosophical mood)
- Intro/outro music? → Suno (catchy, memorable)
- Long-form ambient? → Mubert (continuous, unobtrusive)

### Post-Production

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **CapCut** | Primary editor, captions, effects | Free, powerful, trending templates | Mobile-first (desktop exists) |
| **Descript** | Podcast-style, transcript editing | Edit by text, overdub | $15/mo for good features |
| **Captions app** | Quick caption styling | Best auto-caption styling | Limited editing |

**Decision tree:**
- Full video edit + captions? → CapCut
- Editing by transcript (cut "ums", rearrange)? → Descript
- Just need styled captions fast? → Captions app

### Auto-Clipping (Long-form → Shorts)

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Opus Clip** | Primary choice for viral clips | AI finds viral moments, auto-reframe | $19/mo |
| **Vizard** | Backup, different algorithm | Different clip selection | Less proven |
| **Chopcast** | Podcast/interview content | Good for talking head | Less for visual content |

**Decision tree:**
- YouTube long-form → shorts? → Opus Clip
- Want different clip suggestions? → Run through Vizard too
- Podcast content? → Chopcast

### Distribution & Scheduling

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Typefully** | Twitter threads, scheduling | Best for Twitter, analytics | Twitter-only |
| **Publer** | Multi-platform posting | All platforms, bulk upload | Less Twitter-specific |
| **Later** | Instagram-focused | Visual calendar, IG features | Weaker on Twitter |
| **Repurpose.io** | Auto-distribute one video everywhere | Set and forget, saves hours | $25/mo |

**Decision tree:**
- Twitter content? → Typefully
- YouTube video → all platforms? → Repurpose.io
- Instagram planning? → Later
- Everything else? → Publer

### Email & List Building

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **ConvertKit** | Starting out (<1K subs) | Free tier, creator-focused | Paid tier expensive |
| **Beehiiv** | Newsletter-first, growth features | Referral system, monetization | Newer platform |

**Decision tree:**
- Just starting? → ConvertKit (free)
- Planning newsletter as product? → Beehiiv
- Over 1K subs? → Evaluate both pricing

### Photo Enhancement (Instagram)

| Tool | When to Use | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Remini** | Upscaling, sharpening, old photos | Best AI enhancement | Can over-process |
| **Lightroom** | Color grading, presets, consistency | Professional control | Learning curve |
| **Facetune** | Subtle facial touch-ups | Easy, quick | Easy to overdo |

**Workflow:** Raw photo → Remini (if needed) → Lightroom (color/mood) → Facetune (minimal)

### Thread Templates (from twitter-framework.md)

1. **"How X Actually Works"** — Hook: "Most people think X works like [common belief]. They're wrong:"
2. **"Why Conventional Wisdom Is Wrong"** — Hook: "[Common advice]. This advice keeps you stuck. Here's why:"
3. **"If you're a man in your 20s"** — Hyper-specific audience targeting
4. **Number + Promise** — "7 lessons that took me 10 years to learn:"

### Pre-Publish Checklist

- [ ] Hook creates curiosity gap (doesn't resolve in first tweet)
- [ ] Reading level ≤ 7th grade
- [ ] High-arousal emotion (awe, anger, anxiety, amusement)
- [ ] No semicolons (unretweetable)
