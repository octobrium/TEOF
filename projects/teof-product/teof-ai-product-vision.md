**Date:** 2025-11-30

## The Vision

TEOF protocol is free. This document describes the services layer — how to monetize while keeping the protocol open.

**The protocol:** Free, open, anyone can use/extend/teach
**The services:** Premium AI, personalization, network access, verification

**Analogy:** Bitcoin (protocol) is free. Coinbase, hardware wallets, mining operations (services) monetize.

From reactive to observant. From confused to coherent. From manipulated to navigating.

**One-liner:** "Frameworks for men who want to see clearly and act coherently in a system designed to keep them reactive."

Power/truth analysis — the sharpest edge:
- Differentiated (nobody packages this)

- Timely (distrust is high, people want real analysis)

## Hallucination Mitigation

TEOF's built-in architecture:
- Bounded iteration (human verification at intervals)
- AI as mirror, not oracle (explicit positioning)
- Provenance marking (track generation distance)
- Framework as anchor (decisions checked against 10-layer hierarchy)

**Edge:** Most AI products don't have anti-hallucination built into their philosophy.

---

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Hallucination harms user | "AI is mirror, not oracle" positioning; verification prompts |
| Platform dependency | Build on multiple platforms; own the knowledge base |
| Competitors copy | Framework depth is moat; takes years to replicate |
| Users don't retain | Network effects; community; compounding personalization |
| "Just another AI wrapper" | Lead with content/framework, AI is delivery mechanism |
