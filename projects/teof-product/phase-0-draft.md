## Step 1: Brand & Domain (1-2 hrs)

### Brand Name Decision

**Considerations:**
- Memorable, simple
- Available as .com domain
- Works for TEOF-derived content (frameworks, clarity, observation)
- Not locked to single niche (allows evolution)

**Options to consider:**
- [Your name] — personal brand, most flexible
- [Concept]-based — e.g., ClarityFramework, ObserverMind, etc.
- [Outcome]-based — e.g., SystematicClarity, CoherentLife, etc.

**Action:** Decide brand name → check domain availability

### Domain Registration

- Registrar: Namecheap or Cloudflare (~$12/year)
- Get .com if possible
- Point to email provider

---

## Step 2: Email Setup (30 min)

### Provider

**ConvertKit (recommended):**
- Free tier up to 1,000 subscribers
- Good landing page builder
- Easy automation for lead magnet delivery

**Alternative:** Beehiiv (also free tier, newsletter-focused)

### Setup Steps

1. Create account
2. Connect custom domain (for professional emails)
3. Create welcome sequence (can be simple: 1-3 emails)
4. Set up lead magnet auto-delivery

---

## Step 3: Lead Magnet (2-3 hrs)

### Content

Reference: `projects/lead-magnet-draft.md`

**Core offering:** TEOF-derived framework in digestible PDF

**Options:**
- "The 10-Layer Framework for Life Decisions"
- "How to Think Clearly in a Noisy World"
- "The Observer's Guide to [X]"

### Production

1. Generate/refine content with AI
2. Edit for voice
3. Design in Canva (simple, clean)
4. Export PDF
5. Upload to ConvertKit for auto-delivery

---

## Step 4: Landing Page (1 hr)

### Requirements

- Clear headline (value prop)
- Brief description (what they get)
- Email capture form
- Lead magnet auto-delivery on signup

### Options

- ConvertKit landing page (simplest, integrated)
- Carrd ($19/year, more design control)
- Notion + Super (if you want blog-style)

### Test

- Sign up with your own email
- Verify lead magnet delivers
- Check mobile display
