## Stream 2: AI Trading System

### Concept
AI-augmented swing trading or algorithmic trading system for personal capital deployment.

### Approach Options

| Approach | Complexity | Capital Required | Time Investment | Risk |
|----------|-----------|------------------|-----------------|------|
| AI-assisted manual swing trading | Low | $10k+ | 5-10 hrs/week | Medium |
| Semi-automated signals + manual execution | Medium | $25k+ | 3-5 hrs/week | Medium |
| Fully automated algo trading | High | $50k+ | Setup heavy, then minimal | High |

### Phase 1: Research & Paper Trading (Months 1-3)

Objective: Develop edge, validate strategy, no real capital at risk

Tasks:
- Define strategy (momentum, mean reversion, trend following, etc.)
- Select markets (crypto more inefficient, equities more stable)
- Build AI analysis pipeline (pattern recognition, sentiment, on-chain if crypto)
- Paper trade to validate

Tools:
- TradingView (charting, alerts)
- Python + AI for analysis
- Paper trading account

KPIs:
- Win rate on paper trades
- Risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe)
- Drawdown management

**Phase 2: Small Capital Deployment (Months 3-6)**

Objective: Validate with real money, small position sizes

- Deploy 5-10% of trading capital
- Strict position sizing (1-2% risk per trade)
- Log all trades, analyze with AI
- Iterate strategy based on results

**Phase 3: Scale or Automate (Months 6-12)**

If edge validated:
- Increase position sizes
- Automate execution (if confident in system)
- AI monitors, you audit

If no edge:
- Reduce to hobby/education
- Focus on Stream 1

### Key Principles

- Never trade money you can't lose
- Edge must be validated before scaling
- AI augments analysis, doesn't replace judgment
- This is higher risk than content business — size accordingly
