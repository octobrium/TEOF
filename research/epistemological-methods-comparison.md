## Candidate Methods

### Tier 1: Strongest Evidence

#### 1. Consider the Opposite / Inversion

**Description:** Before accepting a claim, model what you'd observe if it were false.

**Evidence:**
- [Popper's falsifiability (1934)](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/popper/) — foundational in philosophy of science
- [Arkes (1991)](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/232584443_Multiple_Explanation_A_Consider-an-Alternative_Strategy_for_Debiasing_Judgments) — effective across multiple biases
- ["Consider the opposite" research](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0361476X20300096) — reduces confirmation bias, anchoring, hindsight bias
- [Criminal investigation study](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/353908960_Have_you_considered_the_opposite_A_debiasing_strategy_for_judgment_in_criminal_investigation) — effective even with professionals
- **Limitation:** Optimal dosage is ~2 counterarguments, not 10; effectiveness is domain-dependent

**Effect Size:** "Greater corrective effect than demand-laden instructions to be fair and unbiased"

**TEOF Derivation:**
- A3 (observation must refine) — inversion generates comparison
- A4 (contradiction must collapse) — inversion surfaces contradictions
- **Derivation strength:** Moderate — plausible but not unique

**Score:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

#### 2. Probabilistic Thinking / Calibration

**Description:** Assign explicit probability estimates; track accuracy; update on evidence.

**Evidence:**
- [Tetlock's Good Judgment Project](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/1745691615577794) — superforecasters 30% more accurate than intelligence analysts with classified info
- Superforecasters maintained accuracy across 2+ years, hundreds of questions
- [Calibration training](https://marketing.wharton.upenn.edu/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/01.06.2020-Morewedge-Carey-PAPER-DebiasingTransferstotheField.pdf) — effects transfer to field, persist 2-3 months
- Key: explicit probability assignments enable tracking and improvement

**Effect Size:** 30% improvement over baselines in controlled conditions

**TEOF Derivation:**
- A2 (observation persists) — tracking enables accumulation
- A3 (observation must refine) — calibration enables refinement
- **Derivation strength:** Strong — directly implements refinement loop

**Score:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

**Effect Size:** 30% improvement in identifying failure causes; significant overconfidence reduction
