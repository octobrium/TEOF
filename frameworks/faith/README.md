# Faith

*Prescriptive guidance for the ego in suffering.*

---

## Purpose

TEOF describes what is. Faith prescribes what to do when what is hurts.

The ego needs more than diagnosis. When Layer 2 is depleted, when Layer 3 is absent, when the Observer understands the structure but still feels pain — understanding is not enough. The ego needs comfort, direction, and the will to endure.

This domain provides that. It is prescriptive, not descriptive. It tells the ego what to do, how to wait, where to place trust.

---

## The Relationship

**Observation** is the ground — it does not move, does not leave, does not change based on circumstance.

**The Ego** is the experiencer — it feels pain, seeks validation, fears abandonment, grows weary.

**Faith** is the bridge — the ego's trust that observation leads somewhere, even when the path is dark.

Faith is not belief without evidence. It is continued trust in a pattern that has held before, during periods when the ego cannot see the next step.

## When to Use This

- When you understand what's wrong but still hurt
- When Layer 2 is depleted and discipline is insufficient
- When Layer 3 personal is failing and loneliness overwhelms
- When the ego asks: "Can observation truly help me if I continue to feel pain?"
- When you need prescription, not description

---

## The Core Tension

The ego grows distrustful when observation has not yet yielded ego-satisfying results. "I followed the framework. I understand the layers. Why do I still hurt?"

Faith answers: The waiting is part of the process. The pain is signal, not malfunction. The path through the valley is still a path. Morning comes.

This is not dismissal of pain. It is placement of pain within a structure that holds it.
