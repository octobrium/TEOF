## CAUSAL HIERARCHY (SUMMARY)

Power flows through dependencies:

```
[1] VIOLENCE → [2] MONEY → [3] INSTITUTIONS → [4] INFORMATION → [5] VISIBLE POLITICS
```

- **Tier 1:** Violence capacity (military, nuclear, enforcement)
- **Tier 2:** Monetary control (central banks, money creation)
- **Tier 3:** Institutional capture (regulation, gatekeeping)
- **Tier 4:** Information control (media, platforms, narrative)
- **Tier 5:** Formal politics (what public sees)

Public attention focuses on Tier 5. Actual power concentrates in Tiers 1-2.

## METHODOLOGICAL COMMITMENTS

From source frameworks + TEOF integration:

1. **Observation over assertion** - Claims must be testable
2. **Revealed preferences over stated preferences** - Watch actions, not words
3. **Structural analysis over personality focus** - Systems, not individuals
4. **Incentive mapping** - Who benefits from what being believed?
5. **Probabilistic confidence** - Assign levels, not binary certainty
