## EPISTEMIC STANDARDS

This book attempts to maintain intellectual honesty through:

1. **Grounding claims in evidence:** Bank of England publications on money creation, Gilens/Page study on policy outcomes, declassified documents on intelligence operations, etc.

2. **Acknowledging uncertainty:** See Appendix A for explicit discussion of where claims exceed evidence.

3. **Steelmanning opposition:** See Appendix B for the strongest versions of views the book challenges.

4. **Avoiding false precision:** Probability assignments are rough orderings, not calibrated predictions.

5. **Providing sources:** See Appendix C for key references and recommended reading.

6. **Applying the Observer's Method to itself:** The skepticism advocated should be applied to this book as well.

## WRITING STYLE NOTES

The chapters follow the style established in `romance.md`:

1. **Prologue establishes methodology** - What this is, what it isn't, who it's for
2. **Numbered sections with clear hierarchy** - 1.1, 1.2, etc.
3. **Core thesis stated early** in each chapter
4. **Evidence and observation** before prescription
5. **Practical applications** integrated throughout
6. **TEOF Analysis** sections connecting to the framework
7. **Navigation Principles** at chapter end
8. **Summary conclusions** that synthesize the chapter
