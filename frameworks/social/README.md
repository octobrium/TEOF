## CORE PRINCIPLES (from sources)

### From Evolutionary Psychology
- Attraction has evolved triggers (not arbitrary social construction)
- Hypergamy is observable cross-culturally
- Signaling and counter-signaling dynamics

### From Attachment Theory
- Early patterns influence adult relationships
- Secure attachment enables better relationship outcomes
- Anxious/avoidant dynamics are predictable and manageable

### From Social Exchange Theory
- Relationships involve value exchange
- Investment creates commitment
- Alternatives shape leverage

### From Stoicism
- Outcome independence is both ethical and attractive
- Frame control begins with internal state
- Dichotomy of control applies to relationship outcomes

### From Religious Community Practices
- Accountability relationships produce growth
- Brotherhood requires obligation, not just preference
- Shared mission bonds more than shared entertainment

## METHODOLOGICAL COMMITMENTS

From source frameworks + TEOF integration:

1. **Observation over prescription** — What patterns persist, not what should be
2. **Mechanism over moralizing** — How dynamics work, not how they ought to work
3. **Optionality over outcomes** — Build capability, choose configuration later
4. **Long-term over short-term** — Sustainable patterns over immediate wins
5. **Integrity over manipulation** — Genuine development over tactical deception

### Tier 1: AI Image Generators (Create New Photos)

For generating professional headshots, lifestyle photos, or enhanced portraits from selfies:

| Tool | Best For | Realism | Price | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|-------|-------|
| **Midjourney V6+** | Photorealistic portraits | Excellent | $10-30/mo | Best-in-class realism; Discord-based |

### Recommended Stack

**For Instagram revival (your situation):**

1. **Generate new photos:** Midjourney V6 or Flux Pro (lifestyle shots, travel scenes)
2. **Enhance existing photos:** Remini (upscale old photos) + Lensa (portrait touch-ups)
3. **Dating apps:** TinderProfile.ai or manually curate from Midjourney outputs
4. **Quick touch-ups:** Facetune (mobile, fast)

**Workflow:**
```
Selfies (10-20 varied) → AI generator (Midjourney/Flux) →
Enhancement pass (Remini/Lensa) → Platform-specific crops → Post
```

### Caution

- AI-generated photos should represent you accurately
- Dating apps may detect/penalize obvious AI images
- Mix AI-enhanced with genuine photos for authenticity
- Best results: enhance real photos rather than generate fake ones
