## CORE HIERARCHY

Health interventions follow a hierarchy of impact:

```
[1] SLEEP → [2] NUTRITION → [3] EXERCISE → [4] MENTAL HEALTH → [5] ENVIRONMENT/SUPPLEMENTS → [6] METABOLIC OPTIMIZATION
```

- **Tier 1:** Sleep (foundation for all other function)
- **Tier 2:** Nutrition (fuel and building blocks)
- **Tier 3:** Exercise (capacity and adaptation)
- **Tier 4:** Mental Health (psychological optimization)
- **Tier 5:** Environment + Supplements (light, air, hydration, marginal gains)
- **Tier 6:** Metabolic Health (biomarkers, long-term optimization)

Broken sleep makes everything else harder. The hierarchy must be followed in order.

## SOURCE FRAMEWORKS

This framework curates and integrates principles from existing reality-tested sources:

| Source | Domain | What's Borrowed |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Matthew Walker's research | Sleep | Mechanisms, 7-9 hour target, sleep stage importance |
| Andrew Huberman's protocols | Sleep, Light, Supplements | Light exposure timing, supplement stack, circadian optimization |
| Peter Attia's framework | Metabolic health, Longevity | Biomarker targets, exercise programming, zone 2 focus |
| Layne Norton's work | Nutrition, Protein | Protein targets (1g/lb), distribution, evidence-based nutrition |
| Scientific literature | All domains | Specific studies cited throughout (Walker, Nedergaard, Cohen, Cappuccio, etc.) |
| Islamic fasting practices | Meal timing, Discipline | Periodic fasting structure (borrowed implicitly) |

**Note:** This framework synthesizes existing evidence and expert protocols. It does not claim to derive health principles from first principles. TEOF's role is selection and integration, not original research.

See: `/frameworks/sources/` for detailed analysis of why these source frameworks persist.

## METHODOLOGICAL COMMITMENTS

From source frameworks + TEOF integration:

1. **Evidence-based** — Recommendations backed by research (from scientific method)
2. **First principles** — Understanding why, not just what (enables adaptation)
3. **Practical application** — Actionable protocols, not just theory
4. **Individual variation** — Principles apply; details may vary
5. **Hierarchy awareness** — Fundamentals before optimization (from all sources)
