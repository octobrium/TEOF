## KEY FINDINGS AT A GLANCE

### The Core Question
**What structure does energy/evolution naturally select for?**

**Answer: DIVERGENCE, not convergence.**

Different strategies dominate at different scales:
- Plants: Maximum biomass (82% of life)
- Bacteria: Maximum per-gram efficiency (100-1000x)
- Animals: Mobility and flexibility
- Humans: Information processing and tool leverage

### The Great Energy Paradox
- Bacteria process energy 100-1000x more efficiently per gram
- Yet plants capture 6x more total carbon
- Different metrics → different winners
- **Evolution explores orthogonally, not toward single optimum**

### Information Processing Acceleration
- Exponential growth over 3 billion years
- 10^6 → 10^18 bits/gram/sec
- Human brain at theoretical ceiling (~10^15)
- GPUs approaching biological efficiency
- **Room for 1000x more intelligence without hitting physics limits**

### Pattern C: Empirically Universal Attractor
Pattern C (tiered hierarchy + observation-first + distributed control):
- Cells (nucleus + organelles)
- Organisms (brain + distributed reflexes)
- Brains (thalamus/cortex hub + modules)
- Ecosystems (hubs + edges)
- Markets (discovery + regulation)
- Internet (BGP core + edge apps)

**Suggests Pattern C is fundamental for information-processing systems.**

### Hierarchy vs Flat: The Hybrid Solution
- Pure centralized: Brittle, slow
- Pure decentralized: Uncoordinated, slow
- Hybrid (core + edge): Fast, robust, adaptive
- **All successful large-scale systems are hybrid**

### Thermodynamic Limits: Room to Grow
- **Energy budget:** Brains use 0.1% of civilization energy (1000x available)
- **Information processing:** Near ceiling per gram, but vast underutilization
- **Physical substrate:** No known barrier to 1000x denser processing
- **Heat dissipation:** 15 W/cm³ actual vs 1000 W/cm³ theoretical

**We are NOT at physical limits. Bottleneck is engineering, not physics.**

### Convergence or Divergence?
**DIVERGENCE in almost all metrics:**
- Ecological niches: 10 → 10,000+
- Metabolic strategies: 1 → 1000+
- Species diversity: 30 → 5-10 million

**But acceleration in specific dimensions:**
- Information density: Exponential
- Cultural accumulation: Exponential (last 10K years)
- Energy leverage: 10-100x amplification via tools

**Red Queen races persist alongside directional trends.**
